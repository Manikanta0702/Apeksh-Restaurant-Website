Welcome to Apeksh Restaurant

Empowering the Disabled people: Led the design and development of Project "Apeksh" aimed at providing employment opportunities to individuals with hearing and speech impairments. Orchestrated a multidisciplinary team to create an inclusive digital platform, emphasizing accessibility and equal opportunity.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a warm and welcoming environment where everyone can enjoy delicious food while supporting a cause that matters. We strive to provide opportunities for individuals with hearing and speech impairments, ensuring they have a place where they can thrive and contribute meaningfully to society.
